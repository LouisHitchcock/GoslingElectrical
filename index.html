Our Services:

Electrical Installations:

Installing lighting, outlets, switches, and electrical panels. Wiring for new homes or home renovations.

Electrical Repairs:

Fixing faulty wiring, outlets, or light fixtures. Troubleshooting electrical issues like tripped breakers or power surges.

Rewiring:

Upgrading or replacing outdated or unsafe wiring in older homes.

Safety Inspections:

Conducting electrical inspections to ensure compliance with local codes and regulations (EICRS)

Lighting Design and Installation:

Installing various lighting solutions, including ambient, task, and accent lighting.

Circuit Breaker and Fuse Box Services:

Installing and repairing circuit breakers, fuse boxes, and panels to handle electrical load safely.

Electrical Upgrades:

Upgrading electrical systems to accommodate modern appliances and technology.

Home Automation:

Installing smart home devices such as automated lighting, thermostats, or security systems.

Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detector Installation:

Installing and maintaining detectors for fire.

Diagnosing and fixing issues with power supply, malfunctioning outlets, or appliances.
